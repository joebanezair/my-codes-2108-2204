FAQ

  • How do I create private game?

    From the home page click [my private house] - [create game] - [select game category ] - [set game properties], click start now to create success.

  • How to invite friends to join your game?

    From my private room, click [Invite Friends] - [select Invite Mode] - [send the copied text to my friends]. Friends will copy the text, open it to Goplay360, can enter your room.

  • How to share Goplay360 with friends?

    Click to go to my page - [select Share Goplay360] - select the social software to share, and then share with your friends.

  • How to Create a Club?

    Click the upper right corner of the club page +, select [Create Club], fill in the club information, and click [Finish] to create the club.

  • How to create a club game?

    In the club page, click the [Create Game] at the bottom right, select the club, select the game type to be created, and click [Start Now].
